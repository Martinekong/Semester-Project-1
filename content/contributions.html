your contribution matters

support the science museum

At the Science Museum of Norway, we are passionate about making science accessible, exciting, and inspiring for children, families, and the community. With your help, we can continue to create engaging exhibits, offer hands-on learning experiences, and host educational programs that ignite a love of science in young minds.

ways to contribute

make a donation

Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference. You can make a one-time donation or set up a monthly gift to provide ongoing support.

become a member

Join our community of supporters by becoming a museum member. Membership comes with a range of benefits, including exclusive events, and discounts at our museum shop.

sponsor exhibits

If you're a business or organization, consider sponsoring one of our exhibits or educational programs. Sponsorship opportunities are available at various levels.

volunteer time

We welcome volunteers who want to share their time and talents with us. Whether you're interested in helping with events, guiding tours, or assisting in workshops.

we need your help

the hall of innovation

Be a part of the future by contributing to our upcoming exhibition, The Hall of Innovation! This groundbreaking exhibit will explore the world's most impactful inventions, technological advancements, and the creative minds behind them, inspiring a new generation of thinkers and doers.

Contribute your time and expertise by becoming a volunteer. We need enthusiastic individuals to help guide visitors through the exhibits, assist with hands-on activities, and inspire the next generation of innovators.
