your contribution matters

support the science museum of norway

At the Science Museum of Norway, we are passionate about making science accessible, exciting, and inspiring for children, families, and the community. With your help, we can continue to create engaging exhibits, offer hands-on learning experiences, and host educational programs that ignite a love of science in young minds.

ways to contribute

  1. Make a donation: Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference. You can make a one-time donation or set up a monthly gift to provide ongoing support.
  2. Become a member Join our community of supporters by becoming a museum member. Membership comes with a range of benefits, including exclusive events, and discounts at our museum shop. Your membership fees directly support our exhibits and educational programs.
  3. Sponsor an exhibit:If you're a business or organization, consider sponsoring one of our exhibits or educational programs. Sponsorship opportunities are available at various levels and include recognition for your company or organization as a valued supporter of science education.
  4. Volunteer your time: Not all contributions have to be financial! We welcome volunteers who want to share their time and talents with us. Whether you're interested in helping with events, guiding tours, or assisting in workshops, your involvement is invaluable to our mission.
