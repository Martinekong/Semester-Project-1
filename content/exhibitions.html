The Hall of Dinosaurs

Step back in time to the age of giants

The Hall of Dinosaurs at is a journey into a world where giant reptiles ruled the Earth. From the terrifying Tyrannosaurus rex to the gentle, long-necked Brachiosaurus, our exhibits bring these magnificent creatures to life. Whether you're a budding paleontologist or simply fascinated by the prehistoric world, this hall offers an immersive experience like no other.

Dinosaur VR-Experience

Explore the world of the dinosaurs in our VR-experience. Will you survive these ancient creatures?

The Hall to space

Embark on a journey beyond earth

The Hall to Space invites you to explore the final frontier. From the birth of stars to the latest in space exploration, this exhibit is a portal to the wonders of the cosmos. Whether you're an aspiring astronaut or simply captivated by the night sky, our exhibits offer a captivating glimpse into the universe's vast mysteries. Walk among the planets in our detailed scale model.

Rocket Simulator

Travel to space with our rocket simulator. How many planets can you see, and is there any life?

the hall of the animal kingdom

Discover the diversity of life on earth

The Hall of the Animal Kingdom is your gateway to the incredible variety of life that inhabits our planet. From the smallest insects to the largest mammals, this exhibit showcases the wonders of the animal world, exploring the fascinating adaptations, behaviors, and ecosystems that make life on Earth so unique. Whether you're passionate about wildlife or just curious about the natural world, this hall offers an enriching and immersive experience for all ages.

Virtual Safari

Go on a virtual safari and see if you can spot all the animals. From lions, to elephants, to hippos.

the hall of science

Unveil the mysteries of the universe

The Hall of Science is a celebration of human curiosity and innovation. Dive into the world of physics, chemistry, biology, and beyond as you explore the fundamental principles that govern our universe. From groundbreaking discoveries to hands-on experiments, this exhibit offers a dynamic and interactive experience that makes science accessible and exciting for all ages.

Science Lab

Join us in our science lab - the place to explore and learn the wonders of science and chemistry.
