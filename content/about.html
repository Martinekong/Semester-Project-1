about us

dive into a world of discovery

Welcome to the Science Museum of Norway, where curiosity meets discovery! Our museum is dedicated to inspiring the next generation of thinkers, creators, and innovators by making science accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone.

Our mission is to ignite a passion for science and technology in children, families, and the community. We believe that science is for everyone, and we strive to create an environment where visitors of all ages can explore, learn, and be inspired.

What we offer

01

interactive exhibits

Dive into our hands-on exhibits that cover a wide range of scientific topics, from the mysteries of space to the wonders of the animal kingdom. Our exhibits are designed to be both educational and entertaining, making learning an adventure for visitors of all ages.

02

Educational Programs

We offer a variety of programs and workshops that bring science to life. Whether it's a school field trip, a weekend workshop, or a special event, our programs are tailored to inspire and engage young minds.

03

Special Events

Throughout the year, we host special events that explore different aspects of science and technology. From science fairs to guest lectures, there's always something new and exciting happening at the Science Museum of Norway.
